Solution Description (Under 150 words)

Eltizamati helps people in Jordan make confident, money-saving decisions about their loans and credit obligations. Instead of guessing, users see exactly how each payment splits between interest and principal, what an early settlement really costs after penalties, and how every repayment choice changes the total they pay. Users import their obligations by connecting their bank — no tedious manual entry — into a single, clear dashboard with schedules and reminders. Eltizamati's engine automatically detects hidden balloon payments, warns users before they hit, and simulates restructuring options using Jordan-specific lending rules and Shariah-compliant framing. Every number comes with a plain-language explanation, so users understand the reasoning behind each decision. More than a loan calculator, Eltizamati turns opaque loan contracts into clear choices — helping borrowers avoid costly surprises, compare their options side by side, and stay in control of their debt.

Main Functionalities

* Automatic obligation import by connecting the user's bank — no manual data entry.
* Balloon payment detection, early warnings, and restructuring simulation.
* Repayment scenario comparison, including early-settlement cost analysis with penalties.
* Detailed payment breakdown: interest, principal, remaining balance, and repayment progress.
* Centralized dashboard for all loans and credit obligations, with due-date reminders.
* Interest-rate change alerts and impact simulation.
* Debt-burden and affordability insights.
* Plain-language explanations of loan terms, tailored to the Jordanian context.
